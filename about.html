About Us

YogaUnnati was founded to share authentic yogic practices with clarity and commitment.

Rooted in Classical Hatha Yoga, the intention is to offer transformative tools in a simple, approachable, and life-relevant way.

Meet Nikhil

Classical Hatha Yoga Teacher
Certified from Sadhguru Gurukulam

YogaUnnati was founded by Nikhil, a software engineer and Hatha Yoga teacher, who has been practicing Isha yoga since 2019, and teaching yoga since 2023.

His life took a profound turn after he came across Sadhguru's videos, which later led him to attend Isha programs. Deeply transformed by these experiences, Nikhil felt inspired to undergo the teacher training program, and he now dedicates himself to offering these life-changing yogic practices to others.

What We Offer

Programs and spaces designed around steady practice, inner balance, and experiential learning.

Classical Hatha Yoga

Structured offerings in foundational and deeper Hatha Yoga practices for physical, mental, and energetic wellbeing.

Children's Programs

Engaging yoga sessions for children that support focus, emotional balance, discipline, and joyful involvement.

Workshops & Guidance

Short and long-format programs that help participants build consistency and deepen their connection with practice.

Why Yoga Unnati

The intent is to create a meaningful bridge between timeless yogic processes and everyday life. Whether someone is just beginning or looking to deepen their practice, Yoga Unnati aims to offer guidance that is steady, human, and rooted in genuine care.
