Why This Summer Matters

Why Summer Is a Turning Point

Summer vacation is one of the few periods when a child's internal habits can be consciously shaped.

During school months, structure is external. During summer vacation, internal habits are formed.

Without structure, children often drift toward:

  • 6-8 hours of screen time daily
  • Disrupted sleep and routine
  • Scattered attention, harder to focus
  • Emotional reactivity, quick frustration
  • Passive consumption, zero ownership

With the right environment, the same time can help build:

  • Trained attention and focus
  • Emotional steadiness under pressure
  • A daily practice they own themselves
  • Clarity before action, not after
  • Ownership and responsibility

The Children Leadership Journey is designed to create that environment.

Frequently Asked Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Will my child actually show up every day?

Children often settle into the daily rhythm when the timing is fixed and the class is live. Parents are encouraged to support consistency during the first few weeks.

What if my child misses sessions?

Missing an occasional session is fine. The journey is built on long-term consistency, not perfection every single day.

My child has never done yoga. Will they manage?

Yes. The program is designed to support beginners and gradually build familiarity, ease, and confidence over time.

Is this religious?

No. The offering is yogic in nature and focused on inner balance, attention, discipline, and wellbeing.

What device does my child need?

A phone, tablet, or laptop with a stable internet connection and enough space to sit and practice comfortably is sufficient.
