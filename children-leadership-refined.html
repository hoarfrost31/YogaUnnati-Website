Frequently Asked Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Will my child actually show up every day?

Children often settle into the daily rhythm when the timing is fixed and the class is live. Parents can support consistency during the first few weeks.

What if my child misses sessions?

Missing an occasional session is fine. The journey is built on long-term consistency, not perfection every single day.

My child has never done yoga. Will they manage?

Yes. The program is designed to support beginners and gradually build comfort, ease, and confidence.

Is this religious?

No. The offering is yogic in nature and focused on inner balance, attention, discipline, and wellbeing.

What device does my child need?

A phone, tablet, or laptop with stable internet and enough space to sit and practice comfortably is sufficient.
